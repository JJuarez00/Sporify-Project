dawnFM*

A Spotify stats dashboard built as a final project.

Sign in with Spotify to see your top tracks and artists. The search page works without an account.

What this project does

  • View your top tracks and artists up to 4 weeks, 6 months, or all time
  • Search any artist on Spotify and preview their tracks
  • Export your current stats as a PDF

Note: Spotify deprecated the artist top-tracks endpoint for dev-mode apps in February 2026. Artist results on the search page use Spotify Search as a workaround.
